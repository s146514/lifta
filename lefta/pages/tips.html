💡 Financial Tips

Expert advice to help you save more, spend wisely, and build wealth over time.

Top 6 Financial Tips

01

Follow the 50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% of your income to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment. This simple rule provides a balanced framework for budgeting.

02

Build an Emergency Fund

Aim to save 3–6 months of living expenses in a separate, easily accessible account. This protects you from unexpected events like job loss or medical emergencies.

03

Pay Yourself First

Set up automatic transfers to your savings account the moment your paycheck arrives. Treat savings like a non-negotiable expense, not an afterthought.

04

Track Every Purchase

Small expenses add up quickly. Use Lefta to log every transaction, no matter how small, to identify spending patterns and find opportunities to cut back.

05

Eliminate High-Interest Debt

Prioritize paying off high-interest debt like credit cards before investing. A credit card charging 20% interest is a guaranteed 20% loss on your money.

06

Invest Early & Consistently

The power of compound interest means that even small amounts invested early can grow significantly over time. Start with any amount you can afford.

Quick Glossary

  • APR: Annual Percentage Rate — the yearly cost of borrowing
  • Net Worth: Assets minus liabilities
  • Liquidity: How quickly an asset can be converted to cash
  • Compound Interest: Earning interest on your interest
