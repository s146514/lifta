About FinTrack

FinTrack is a personal finance management web application designed and developed as part of the COMP3700 (Introduction to Web Computing) course project at Sultan Qaboos University during Spring 2026.

Our mission is to make personal finance management simple, accessible, and effective for everyone — especially students and young professionals who are beginning their financial journey.

The platform covers all essential aspects of personal finance including budgeting, expense tracking, savings goal management, and loan calculations.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to take control of their financial lives through an intuitive and comprehensive web-based tool that is completely free to use.

Project Highlights

  • Built using pure HTML and CSS — no frameworks
  • Fully responsive across all screen sizes
  • 8 complete web pages with full functionality
  • Deployed on GitHub Pages and a free hosting platform
  • Collaborative development via GitHub
  • Compliant with COMP3700 project requirements
