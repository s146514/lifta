💡 Loan Tips

  • Making extra payments reduces your total interest significantly.
  • Compare interest rates from multiple banks before committing.
  • A shorter loan term means higher monthly payments but less total interest.
  • Always read the fine print for any hidden fees or early repayment penalties.
  • Use the 28/36 rule: housing costs ≤ 28% and total debt ≤ 36% of gross income.
