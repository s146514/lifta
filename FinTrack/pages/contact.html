Frequently Asked Questions

Is FinTrack free to use?

Yes, FinTrack is completely free. No account registration or payment is required.

Can I use it on my phone?

Absolutely! FinTrack is fully responsive and works on all screen sizes including smartphones and tablets.

Is my data saved?

This is Part 2 of the project (static site). Data is not stored permanently yet; that will be added in Part 3 with JavaScript and PHP.
